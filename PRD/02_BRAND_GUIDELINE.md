# 02. Brand Guideline

## Brand Identity
- Name: CodeStory
- Tagline: Build Websites That Grow Your Business.
- Brand personality: premium, modern, technical, trustworthy, minimal, future-facing

## Visual Direction
The visual style should feel like a premium startup website, inspired by modern product companies rather than generic agency templates.

## Color Palette
- Primary: #2563EB
- Dark: #0F172A
- Background: #F8FAFC
- Secondary text: #64748B
- Border: #E2E8F0
- Accent glow: rgba(37, 99, 235, 0.35)

## Background Treatment
Do not use a plain white background everywhere. Use:
- subtle gradients,
- aurora glow effects,
- soft blue neon lighting,
- faint grid overlays,
- occasional dark sections for contrast.

## Typography
- Headings: Sora
- Body: Inter
- Code / terminal: JetBrains Mono

## Logo Usage
- Keep the logo small and clean in the navbar.
- Use the official logo exactly as provided.
- Do not distort or replace the logo.

## Imagery
- Use product-style mockups
- Use abstract motion backgrounds
- Use portfolio previews with realistic UI frames

## Tone of Voice
- Direct
- Confident
- Professional
- Friendly
- Conversion-focused

## Motion Style
Motion should feel premium, smooth, and restrained.
Recommended behavior:
- soft fade and rise transitions,
- staggered text entrance,
- gentle parallax,
- glow hover states,
- terminal-style opening animation.
