# 18. Copywriting

## Objective
Provide ready-to-use marketing copy that matches the premium positioning of CodeStory.

## Brand Headline Options
- Build Websites That Grow Your Business.
- Website yang Tidak Hanya Indah, Tapi Juga Menghasilkan Klien.
- Premium Websites for Modern Businesses.
- Solusi Website untuk Bisnis yang Ingin Naik Level.

## Hero Copy
### English
Headline: Build Websites That Grow Your Business.
Subtitle: We design and develop modern websites that help businesses look credible, load fast, and convert better.

### Indonesian
Headline: Website yang Tidak Hanya Indah, Tapi Juga Menghasilkan Klien.
Subtitle: Kami membangun website modern yang cepat, profesional, dan membantu bisnis Anda mendapatkan lebih banyak pelanggan.

- Tell us about your project

## FAQ Copy
Suggested questions:
- What is included in each package?
- How long does the project take?
- Do you provide revisions?
- Can you help with hosting and domain?
- Can the website be updated later?

## Tone Rules
- Keep sentences confident and concise
- Avoid overly technical language for public pages
- Focus on outcomes and trust
- Use direct calls to action

## Acceptance Criteria
All public-facing copy should sound premium, clear, and conversion-oriented in both Indonesian and English.
