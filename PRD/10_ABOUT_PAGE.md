# 10. About Page

## Objective
Explain who CodeStory is, what it stands for, and why clients should trust the brand.

## Page Structure
1. Hero
2. Story Section
3. Mission and Vision
4. Values
5. Timeline
6. Strengths
7. CTA

## Hero
Headline: "Built for Businesses That Want to Stand Out"
Subtitle: A short introduction about CodeStory as a premium web development agency.

## Story Section
Explain:
- why CodeStory was created,
- the focus on helping businesses grow through better websites,
- the emphasis on quality, speed, and modern design.

## Mission
- Build websites that look premium and perform well.
- Help clients turn visitors into customers.
- Provide a smooth and reliable development experience.

## Vision
Become a trusted web development brand for businesses in Indonesia and beyond.

## Values
- Quality first
- Clear communication
- Business-oriented thinking
- Design precision
- Technical reliability

## Timeline
Show the evolution of CodeStory:
- early idea
- first projects
- service refinement
- premium branding direction
- future growth

## Strengths
- Premium UI/UX
- Responsive design
- SEO-minded builds
- Bilingual support
- Private owner dashboard

## CTA
Headline: "Let’s Build Something Meaningful"
Buttons:
- Konsultasi Gratis
- WhatsApp

## Acceptance Criteria
The About page should build trust, explain the brand clearly, and support the main goal of converting visitors into leads.
